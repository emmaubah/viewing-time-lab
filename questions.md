## Context Setting
- How was the A/B test set up, and were employees divided into groups at random?
- Were there any missing data, technological problems, or early departures by employees?

## Goal Alignment
- For stakeholders, what does success mean—a longer watching duration, a higher completion rate, or improved learning outcomes?
- Do stakeholders prioritize knowledge retention above engagement?
- How will the findings be applied to the design of future workshops?

## Define Over-performance
- Would it still be deemed successful if workers watched more but learned less?
- Would redesigning all upcoming presentations be justified by over-performance?

## Data Sourcing
- Is it possible to integrate staff survey responses with viewing time data?
- In addition to watching minutes, do we also have completion-rate data?
